1. Original

The pure, minimal mark you started with.

2. The Margin

Adds a classic faint red margin line. Instantly anchors the composition as a physical piece of planner paper.

3. The Time Arc

Bends the three dots into an arc—symbolizing the passage of a day (morning, noon, evening sun).

4. The Threadline

A faint horizontal line intersecting the dots—a literal interpretation of a timeline or "thread".

5. The Checkmark

Replaces the final dot with a hand-drawn checkmark, shifting the mood from "abstract" to "productivity."
